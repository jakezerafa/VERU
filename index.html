Why cook with
beef tallow?

For centuries, beef tallow was the cooking fat of choice. Modern food science swapped it out for seed oils — but the old ways had it right.

High Smoke Point

Stable above 200°C — perfect for roasting, frying, and searing without oxidising or releasing harmful compounds.

No Seed Oils

Free from inflammatory vegetable and seed oils. Pure rendered fat — nothing added, nothing removed.

Vitamins A, D, E & K

Naturally rich in fat-soluble vitamins your body actually absorbs — especially when paired with whole foods.

Exceptional Flavour

Adds a rich, savoury depth to roast potatoes, vegetables, and meat that no plant-based oil can replicate.

Long Shelf Life

Naturally shelf-stable for months without refrigeration or preservatives. Rendered fat keeps the way nature intended.

Conjugated Linoleic Acid

A naturally occurring fatty acid in grass-fed beef fat, linked to reduced inflammation and a healthy metabolism.

Natural Skin Moisturiser

Tallow's fatty acid profile closely mirrors human sebum — making it one of the most biocompatible moisturisers for skin, without synthetic emollients.

Skin-Safe & Cosmetic Grade

Pure enough to eat, gentle enough for your face. VERU tallow contains no fragrances, no parabens, and no synthetics — just rendered fat as nature made it.
